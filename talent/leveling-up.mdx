## The Operator Ladder

Startery isn’t just a marketplace. It’s a career accelerator with a clear path from entry-level work to strategic leadership.

Each level unlocks more pay, responsibility, and ownership.

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Apprentice" icon="seedling">
    Typically a high school grad or early undergrad.
    <br />
    Pay capped at **$20/hr**.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Analyst" icon="chart-simple">
    Typically a college junior or senior.
    <br />
    Pay capped at **$30/hr**.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Associate" icon="user-graduate">
    Typically a bachelor’s graduate.
    <br />
    Pay capped at **$37.50/hr**.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Project Manager (PM)" icon="people-group">
    1–2 years experience. Leads teams. Can bring in clients.
    <br />
    Pay capped at **$55/hr**.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Engagement Manager (EM)" icon="person-chalkboard">
    3–4 years experience. Leads client engagements and PMs.
    <br />
    Pay capped at **$100/hr**.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Partner" icon="star">
    5+ years experience. Leads verticals. Uncapped pay.
    <br />
    Includes **profit share** in Startery.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Elder / Founding Partner" icon="hands-holding-circle">
    Advisor or owner. Helps guide Startery’s mission and long-term vision.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

<Note>
  All Operators start at a level based on their past experience and their
  performance in Launchpad.
</Note>

## How to Level Up

<Steps>
<Step title="Do Great Work">
Hit deadlines. Deliver excellence. Be a strong communicator and team member.

<Check>
Feedback from clients and peers is factored into level evaluations.
</Check>
</Step>

<Step title="Lead Projects">
Step into PM or EM roles to manage scope, delegate work, and mentor others.

<Tip>
Leading a team well is one of the fastest ways to grow.
</Tip>
</Step>

<Step title="Bring in Clients">
As a PM or EM, you can refer clients and earn commission. Show strategic leadership and you’ll level up faster.
</Step>
</Steps>
