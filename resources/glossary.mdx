## Common Terms

<AccordionGroup>

<Accordion title="Agent">
  An AI-powered tool that helps scope projects, manage timelines, and assist
  human Operators or clients with workflows.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Analyst">
  An intermediate Operator—typically a college junior or senior—skilled enough
  to work on independent deliverables with guidance from a PM or Associate.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Apprentice">
  An entry-level Operator, often a college freshman/sophomore or recent high
  school grad. Works under guidance and earns experience on smaller project
  tasks.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Associate">
  A skilled Operator—usually a recent graduate or early-career professional—who
  can independently handle project deliverables and collaborate within Guilds.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Benefits">
  Startery Operators unlock perks based on their level, including pay raises,
  healthcare access (via Healos), workspace stipends, mentorship, and
  profit-sharing.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Bounty">
  A posted opportunity for a small, specific task. Any accepted Operator can
  claim and complete it for payment.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Client">
  A startup, nonprofit, or organization that hires Operators or Guilds for
  project work. Clients pay for bundles or tracks and collaborate with Operators
  through the platform.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Engagement Manager (EM)">
  A senior Operator (3–5 years experience) who leads client relationships and
  ensures project success by managing strategy and delegating execution to PMs
  and Guilds.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Founding Partner / Elder / Partner Emeritus">
  Experienced leaders who’ve helped shape Startery’s mission. Often act as
  stewards, advisors, or vision holders across the ecosystem.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Guild">
  A small team of Operators working together on a client project. Usually
  cross-functional (e.g., Designer + Developer + PM).
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Launchpad">
  The onboarding sprint where applicants are vetted and assessed through real
  project simulations. Only the top 1% advance to become paid Operators.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Operator">
  A vetted person in the Startery community who takes on paid project work.
  Includes all levels, from Apprentice to Partner.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Operator Levels">
  A structured path that defines an Operator’s pay, scope of work, and
  leadership opportunities—ranging from Apprentice to Partner.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Partner">
  An Operator with 5+ years experience who takes leadership over divisions,
  clients, or communities within Startery. Responsible for strategy, growth, and
  mentoring others.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Project Manager (PM)">
  A mid-level Operator (1–2 years experience) who leads Guilds, oversees
  execution, manages deadlines, and ensures client deliverables are met.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Scoping Call">
  A session between a client and Startery team (usually PM or EM) to define
  project goals, deliverables, timelines, and pricing.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Tracks">
  Startery's four main skill categories: Strategy, Design, Technology, and
  Growth. Operators specialize in one or more tracks for project assignments.
</Accordion>

</AccordionGroup>
