## Common Terms

<AccordionGroup>

<Accordion title="Operator">
  A vetted person in the Startery community who takes on paid project work.
  Includes all levels, from Apprentice to Partner.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Guild">
  A small team of Operators working together on a client project. Usually
  cross-functional (e.g., Designer + Developer + PM).
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Launchpad">
  The onboarding sprint where applicants are vetted and assessed through real
  project simulations.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Agent">
  An AI- or human-powered tool that helps scope projects, manage timelines, and
  assist Operators or clients with workflows.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Partner">
  An Operator with 5+ years experience who takes leadership over divisions,
  clients, or communities within Startery.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Bounty">
  A posted opportunity for a small, specific task. Any accepted Operator can
  claim and complete it for payment.
</Accordion>

</AccordionGroup>
