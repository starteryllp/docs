## Plug in Your Pipeline

If you're a Partner with a network of founders or teams that need strategy, design, or development—you can bring them into Startery.

We handle the delivery. You earn a cut.

---

## How It Works

<Steps>
  <Step title="1. Refer or Scope">
    Send us a lead, or collaborate with our scoping team to turn it into a
    workable project.
  </Step>
  <Step title="2. Team Assembly">
    We staff the project with vetted Operators. You can stay involved as a PM or
    EM—or step back and let us manage.
  </Step>
  <Step title="3. Revenue Split">
    You receive a commission from project revenue, based on your role and
    involvement.
  </Step>
</Steps>
