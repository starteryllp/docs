## Join as a Partner

Startery isn’t just for early talent. We’re building an ecosystem with leaders, mentors, and agency operators who want to build better work models.

Whether you're a freelancer, consultant, agency founder, or domain expert, you can apply to become a Startery Partner.

## The Application Process

<Steps>
<Step title="1. Submit Interest">
Tell us who you are, what you’ve done, and how you’d like to contribute.
</Step>

<Step title="2. Discovery Call">
  We’ll discuss where you fit—leading a vertical, mentoring Operators, joining
  projects, or bringing in clients.
</Step>

<Step title="3. Partnership Setup">
Once accepted, you’ll be onboarded into our Partner system, given access to Operator pools, and shown how to engage.
</Step>
</Steps>
