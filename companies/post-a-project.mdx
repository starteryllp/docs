## Start Working in Days, Not Months

Hiring shouldn’t be this hard. Startery lets you skip the sourcing slog and go straight to outcomes.

Here’s how to post a project.

<Steps>
<Step title="1. Describe Your Need">
Fill out our short project intake form. Share your goals, timeline, and deliverables. No jargon required.
</Step>

<Step title="2. We Scope It">
  Our team (or one of our Scoping Agents) will refine your request into a scoped
  project with timeline, milestones, and proposed talent mix.
</Step>

<Step title="3. We Staff It">
  We match the best team of vetted Operators from our ecosystem—usually within
  48–72 hours.
</Step>

<Step title="4. You Approve and Launch">
Once you approve the scope and team, we kick off. You get updates along the way and can review progress at key milestones.
</Step>
</Steps>

<Check>
  Ready to get started? [Post your project here](https://startery.io).
</Check>
